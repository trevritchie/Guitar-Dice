How It Works

Guitar Dice generates random exercises to help you:

  • Practice chord voicings across the entire fretboard
  • Master chord qualities from Barry Harris' system
  • Learn Thomas Echol's "elevator" voicings
  • Target specific chord tones
  • Break out of familiar patterns
